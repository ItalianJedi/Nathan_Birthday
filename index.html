NATHAN GUMMY BEAR

A New Age

It is a period of todderling. Nathan Gummy Bear, striking from a hidden base, has won his first victory against the evil Galactic Empire...aka, his Parents.

During the battle, Gummy Bear managed to steal secret plans to the Parent’s ultimate weapon, the DEATH STAR, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet and also makes you get ready for bedtime.

Pursued by the Parent’s sinister agent named Plants the Pupper, Gummy Bear races home aboard his starship Jejomar, custodian of the stolen plans that can save his people and restore freedom to his household…
